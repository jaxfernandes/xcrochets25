Our Story

About Xcrochets

Xcrochets was born in 2025 from a quiet passion — a hook, some yarn, and an endless love for creating things by hand. What started as a personal hobby blossomed into a small, passionate studio dedicated to bringing handcrafted crochet art to the world.

Based in Mangalore, every single piece we create is made by hand — with care, creativity, and a deep respect for the craft. We believe that handmade is always more meaningful, that no two pieces should ever be exactly alike, and that a well-crafted object carries a little bit of soul.
