Xcrochets

Common Questions

FAQ

Everything you need to know before ordering.

How do I place an order? +
Simply add items to your cart and click "Send Order on WhatsApp", or use the Bespoke form above. We'll confirm everything directly via WhatsApp, discuss details, and get started on your piece!
How long does it take to make my order? +
Most pieces take 3–7 days depending on complexity. Custom orders may take a little longer. We'll give you a clear timeline when you order, and keep you updated throughout via WhatsApp.
Do you deliver outside Mangalore? +
Yes! We deliver across India. Shipping costs and timelines vary by location. Let us know where you're based and we'll give you full details on WhatsApp.
Can I fully customise a piece? +
Absolutely. That's one of our specialities! Choose your colours, size, character, theme, and more. Use the Bespoke form to describe your vision and we'll bring it to life.
How much do the items cost? +
Pricing varies based on the item, size, and customisation. We share prices personally on WhatsApp to ensure fairness and transparency. Just message us and we'll send you all the details!
Are the crochet classes really free? +
Yes! The first few sessions are completely free of charge. After that, ongoing classes are very affordable and negotiable. Message us on WhatsApp to check availability and schedule your first class.
What makes a crochet gift special? +
A handmade crochet gift carries time, effort and love in every stitch. It's unique — no two pieces are identical. Recipients treasure them for years. For custom character plushies, personalised colours or special occasions, a crochet gift is something truly memorable.
